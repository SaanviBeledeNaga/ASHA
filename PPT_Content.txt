SLIDE 2 — PROBLEM STATEMENT
================================================================================

Title: The Healthcare Gap in Rural India

Key Problems:

• ASHA workers handle 25+ households each, with massive manual paperwork
  — paper registers cause critical data loss and delays.

• India has ~10 lakh (1 million) ASHA workers. Most have limited digital
  literacy and struggle with complex mobile apps.

• Language barriers: Workers speak Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, or local dialects.
  Existing apps are English-only.

• High-risk pregnancies are frequently MISSED because manual tracking
  cannot flag danger signs in real time.

• 65% of rural India faces intermittent or no internet connectivity,
  making cloud-only solutions unusable.

• Delayed healthcare response: Paper-based reporting takes days/weeks
  to reach supervisors — too late for critical interventions.

Statistics:
  → India's Maternal Mortality Ratio: 97 per 100,000 live births (SRS 2020)
  → 44,000+ maternal deaths annually in India
  → ~35% of pregnant women in rural India receive fewer than 4 ANC visits
  → Only 58% of deliveries happen in health facilities in remote areas
  → ASHA workers serve as the ONLY healthcare touchpoint for 80%+ rural families

SLIDE 4 — PROPOSED SOLUTION
================================================================================

Title: Introducing ASHA Copilot AI

"ASHA Copilot is an AI-powered, multilingual healthcare assistant
that enables ASHA workers to record patient visits through voice,
automatically extract clinical data using NLP, detect high-risk
cases in real time, access medical guidelines instantly, and
operate seamlessly in offline rural environments."

Core Solution Pillars:

  1. VOICE-BASED DATA ENTRY
     → ASHA workers speak naturally in Hindi, Telugu, or English.
     → AI transcribes and extracts structured medical data automatically.
     → No typing, no forms — just talk.

  2. AI-POWERED RISK DETECTION
     → Clinical rules engine evaluates blood pressure, symptoms, age factors.
     → Automatically flags high-risk pregnancies (pre-eclampsia, eclampsia).
     → Instant alerts sent to supervisors.

  3. INTELLIGENT GUIDELINE ASSISTANT (RAG)
     → Retrieval-Augmented Generation chatbot for medical queries.
     → Trained on official MoHFW, WHO, UNICEF maternal/child guidelines.
     → ASHA workers get instant answers: "What to do if baby has fever after vaccine?"

  4. OFFLINE-FIRST ARCHITECTURE
     → Records visits locally when offline.
     → Automatically syncs when connectivity is restored.
     → Zero data loss, even in zero-network villages.

  5. SUPERVISOR DASHBOARD
     → Real-time KPIs: total registrations, high-risk cases, active alerts.
     → Village-wise analytics and risk distribution charts.
     → Critical alert management with resolve workflow.

  6. GOVERNMENT SCHEME MATCHING
     → Automatically matches beneficiaries to eligible schemes:
        PMMVY, JSY, PMSMA, Anganwadi Nutrition, ICDS Immunization.

SLIDE 5 — KEY FEATURES
================================================================================

Title: Feature Highlights

✅ Voice-to-Text Visit Recording
   Multilingual speech recognition (Hindi, Telugu, English) converts
   spoken narratives into structured medical records.

✅ AI Clinical Data Extraction (NLP)
   LLM-powered entity extraction: name, age, BP, pregnancy month,
   symptoms, weight — all parsed from natural speech.

✅ High-Risk Pregnancy Detection
   Rules engine checks: hypertension (BP ≥ 140/90), danger symptoms
   (bleeding, convulsions, blurred vision), age factors (teen/advanced).

✅ Beneficiary Tracking & Registry
   Complete maternal registry with risk classification, village mapping,
   pregnancy timeline tracking, and visit history.

✅ Offline-First Sync Engine
   Local SQLite storage → automatic batch sync to server when online.
   Offline simulation toggle built into the UI for demo.

✅ Multilingual Support
   Supports Hinglish, Teluglish, and English transcripts.
   Regex-based fallback NLP works without internet.

✅ RAG-Powered Guideline Assistant
   AI chatbot grounded in official healthcare guidelines.
   Topics: vaccination reactions, anemia prevention, danger signs,
   breastfeeding, newborn warmth care, child malnutrition (SAM/MAM).

✅ Supervisor Dashboard
   KPI cards, regional registration charts, risk profile distribution,
   active critical alerts with one-click resolution.

✅ Government Scheme Recommendations
   Auto-matches: PM Matru Vandana Yojana, Janani Suraksha Yojana,
   PMSMA, Anganwadi Nutrition, ICDS Immunization.

✅ Smart Alerts & Reminders
   Automated high-risk alerts pushed to supervisor dashboard.
   Tracks unresolved alerts until clinical action is taken.

SLIDE 8 — WORKFLOW / USER JOURNEY
================================================================================

Title: How an ASHA Worker Uses ASHA Copilot

Step 1 → LOGIN
  ASHA worker opens the app and logs in with their credentials.

Step 2 → RECORD VISIT VIA VOICE
  Worker clicks the microphone button on the ASHA Companion Portal.
  Speaks naturally: "Lakshmi Devi, age 23, Kothapalli village.
  7th month pregnant. BP 145/95. Pair me swelling hai aur headache..."

Step 3 → AI EXTRACTS MEDICAL DATA
  The NLP engine transcribes and parses the speech into structured fields:
  Name: Lakshmi Devi | Age: 23 | Pregnancy Month: 7
  BP: 145/95 | Symptoms: Swelling, Severe Headache | Weight: 58 kg

Step 4 → RISK ENGINE EVALUATES
  Clinical rules engine detects:
  ⚠️ Severe Hypertension (BP 145/95 ≥ 140/90)
  ⚠️ High-risk symptoms: Swelling + Severe Headache
  → Risk Level: HIGH
  → Recommendation: "URGENT — Refer immediately to nearest PHC/CHC"

Step 5 → GOVERNMENT SCHEMES MATCHED
  Auto-matched eligible schemes:
  → PM Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)
  → Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)
  → Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)
  → Anganwadi Nutrition Support

Step 6 → VISIT SAVED & ALERTS TRIGGERED
  Visit log saved to database.
  HIGH-RISK ALERT automatically pushed to supervisor dashboard.

Step 7 → SUPERVISOR REVIEWS
  Supervisor sees the alert on their dashboard with full clinical context.
  Can resolve the alert after arranging referral transport.

Step 8 → GUIDANCE ON DEMAND
  Worker opens the RAG assistant: "What are danger signs in pregnancy?"
  Gets official guideline-grounded answer instantly.

Title: Scalability & Future Roadmap

🔮 PHASE 2 ENHANCEMENTS:

  1. Wearable Integration
     → Connect with low-cost BP monitors, pulse oximeters, and
        glucometers via Bluetooth for automated vital capture.

  2. Predictive Analytics with ML
     → Train models on historical visit data to predict
        high-risk pregnancies weeks before clinical manifestation.

  3. Government API Integration
     → Direct integration with Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM),
        Health ID, and DigiLocker for verified health records.

  4. Telemedicine Module
     → Enable video/audio consultations between ASHA workers
        and PHC doctors directly within the app.

  5. Regional Language Expansion
     → Add Tamil, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, Odia, Gujarati
        speech recognition and NLP extraction support.

  6. AI Chatbot in Regional Languages
     → Voice-in, voice-out guideline assistant for workers
        who cannot read text responses.

  7. OCR for Handwritten Records
     → Digitize existing paper registers using camera-based
        OCR to bootstrap the digital database.

  8. Flutter Mobile App
     → Native Android/iOS app with offline SQLite, push
        notifications, and camera-based document scanning.

  9. Multi-ASHA Collaboration
     → Enable multiple ASHA workers in a block to share
        beneficiary data and coordinate referrals.

  10. Immunization Tracker
     → Complete child vaccination schedule tracking with
        automated SMS/WhatsApp reminders to mothers.
